Mexican Rice

Mexican rice in a bowl ready to eat

Description

Mexican Rice is a quick and easy recipe. It takes little preperation can be completed in 30 minutes. This recipe has many variations and can be adjusted to taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/3 onion
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cups of water
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil
  • Adobo seasoning

Steps

  1. Blend 2 tomatoes, 1/3 onion, and 2 cloves
  2. Add cooking oil to pan at medium heat
  3. Add rice and cook till rice begins to brown. Stir occasically.
  4. Add blend to rice and stir ocassionlly
  5. Add 2 cups of water. Stir occacailly.
  6. Add 2 spoons of Adobo seasoning
  7. Once it beigins to boil consistently, cover pan and turn heat to simmer
  8. Turn heat off after 10-15 minutes and let it rest for 10 minutes
