Peanut Butter & Jelly

Penanut butter and jelly sandwitch ready to eat

Description

This PB&J recipe is simple and easy to make. It can be done with little-to-no effort. It only requires 3 ingredients and can be completed in less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one side of a slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on one side of the other piece of bread
  3. Put the slices of bread together and enjoy
