Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a quick and easy breakfast that can be completed in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Optional: pepper

Steps

  1. Set pan to medium-low heat
  2. Once heated, add cooking oil
  3. A couple seconds later, add eggs
  4. Stir occasically
  5. Add salt (and pepper) to taste
  6. Once eggs are cooked to desired state, move to plate and enojoy
